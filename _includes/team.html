John Summers

Founder/Owner

John is a Las Vegas native who has worked in the entertainment industry for the last 16 years. As a professional musician, John has worked with a variety of musicians, artists, and entertainers across the world. John founded LV Premium Entertainment to bring his experience with the top talent in the industry to a variety of clients desiring live entertainment for their events. Due to his professional activities, John has had the opportunity to build a unique database of connections that constitute the very best of what Las Vegas entertainment has to offer. He is excited at the prospect of serving all of your entertainment needs.
