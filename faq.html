Frequently Asked Questions

Here's a list of some of our most asked questions. If you have a question that isn't on this list, however, no problem! Feel free to contact us directly. We are always happy to answer your questions.

1) Can't I just search for band myself? Why do I need someone to search for me?

You can definitely try, but we don't recommend it. Unfortunately, there are a lot of independent operators out there that put their information online to try and attract these kinds of jobs. There's no guarantee of quality, or that the person/group that you pick will even show up. We are always sad to hear this, but it does happen sometimes with the random internet groups.

With our contracting process, we protect your interests and ours from these potential pitfalls. We have the experience to weed out the charlatans from the true professionals and tolerate nothing less than the best from the people that we hire.

2) Isn't a live group expensive? Why can't I just use a playlist on iTunes.

Live people performing for you is definitely going to be more expensive than curating a playlist on iTunes Music, but it doesn't have to break the bank. There are options for a wide variety of budgets. Give us a call. We are committed to finding something in your price range that you're going to love.

3) How much time is included in the prices you quote? Do I have to pay extra if I want more time on the day of the event?

The amount of time included in our quotes depends on how much time you need the group/person for. Setup and breakdown are included in the prices we quote. If you want extra time on the day of the event, we do charge extra. The best price is always given when we know how much time you need in advance.

4) I'm using an event planner for my function; will you coordinate with them or do I have to act as a go between?

We will certainly coordinate with them. We want to make sure that this process is as stress free as possible for you. If you provide us with their contact info, we will happily oblige. Just make sure to let them know that you are using someone separate for the music services to eliminate any confusion.

5) What styles of music can you provide?

We are experienced in a wide variety of musical styles and are confident we can accommodate any specific music requests that you may have.

6) What do your groups wear for events?

This depends on a couple of things. Musicians typically will have a few standard options for attire. We can also accommodate special requests. If you have a specific request for apparel, let us know and we will do our best to honor it.
